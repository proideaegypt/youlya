- Updated dark/light mode to match Smart Home charcoal/pink style
- Preserved Supabase SSR auth, login, business logic, APIs
- Updated Playwright swarm to cover all 9 dashboard routes
- Added shadcn/ui components: dropdown-menu, avatar, switch
